The Haunted Corn Maze

About the Corn Maze

  • It is a twist on the original corn maze, with spooky stuff inside to frighten you,
  • including ghosts, skeletons, clowns, werewolves, ax murderers, and other Halloween stereotypes.
  • The maze is usually held at night,
  • and creatures and monsters hide in the corn to scare and chase anyone who comes near them.

What to expect?

People dressed up as Halloween stereotypes, such as clowns, werewolves, and ax murderers, hiding in the corn and jumping out to scare and chase anyone who comes near them.
Ghosts and demons in a witch's house, an abandoned sanitarium, and a vampire crypt.
A terrifying tram ride through the deep woods, complete with werewolves and zombies.
